Lesson 2 20-25 minutes

The Evolution of Money

Money has evolved dramatically throughout human history, from simple barter systems to complex digital currencies. Understanding this evolution is crucial for grasping the revolutionary potential of blockchain technology.

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Trace the evolution of money from barter to cryptocurrency
  • Understand the problems with traditional financial systems
  • Learn how blockchain solves these problems
  • Explore the future of digital currency

The Timeline of Money

Barter System (Prehistoric)

Direct exchange of goods and services. Limited by the "double coincidence of wants" problem.

Commodity Money (3000 BCE)

Gold, silver, and other precious metals became universal mediums of exchange.

Paper Money (7th Century CE)

Representative money backed by precious metals, later becoming fiat currency.

Digital Banking (1950s)

Electronic transfers and digital records of money movement.

Cryptocurrency (2009)

Bitcoin introduces decentralized, trustless digital currency.

Key Innovations of Cryptocurrency

  • Decentralization: No single authority controls the network
  • Immutability: Transactions cannot be altered once recorded
  • Transparency: All transactions are publicly verifiable
  • Security: Cryptographic protection against fraud
  • Accessibility: Anyone with internet can participate
