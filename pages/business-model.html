Executive Summary

BlockZone Lab is a professional blockchain gaming platform that combines competitive gaming with educational content. Our dual revenue model generates sustainable income through championship cycles (primary revenue) and educational subscriptions (secondary revenue), while driving user acquisition through viral friend challenges.

Built on Sonic Labs blockchain infrastructure, we offer 400,000+ TPS, 1-second finality, and 90% fee sharing. The platform currently serves active users through our Neon Drop game and comprehensive blockchain academy, with plans to expand to multiple games and advanced educational content.
