Executive Summary

BlockZone Lab is a professional blockchain gaming platform built on Sonic Labs infrastructure, designed to showcase the capabilities of high-performance blockchain technology while providing sustainable revenue through gaming and educational content.

Our platform leverages Sonic Labs' 400,000+ TPS and 1-second finality to create a seamless gaming experience with real-time micro-payments, championship cycles, and viral friend challenges. The technical architecture ensures scalability, security, and user experience while maintaining the integrity of competitive gaming and educational content delivery.
